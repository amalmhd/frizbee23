About

FrizBee'23

Frizbee'23, the annual national level flagship event of IEEE Student Branch of MES College of Engineering Kuttippuram. Frizbee'23 is a celebration of innovation and creativity, bringing together the brightest minds from across the country to showcase their technical and non-technical skills. With a plethora of events, competitions and workshops, this is a one-of-a-kind platform that provides a glimpse into the future of technology. This year, Frizbee'23 promises to be bigger and better than ever before, with a lineup of exciting events.

Workshops

Lets explore NFT and WEB3

The NFT workshop is a hands-on experience focused on creating and binding NFTs to the web3 platform. Participants will learn about NFTs, blockchain technology, smart contracts, and digital wallets. They will create unique digital assets and mint them as NFTs. The workshop emphasizes the potential applications of NFTs and various marketplaces for buying and selling them. By the end, participants will have a deep understanding of NFTs and their decentralized world.

Digital fabrication laboratory

The digital fabrication workshop is a hands-on learning experience that introduces participants to the world of digital fabrication, also known as 3D printing. The workshop is designed to teach participants how to create 3D models using digital software and how to turn those models into physical objects using 3D printers. Participants will learn about the various tools and software used in digital fabrication, as well as the different types of 3D printers and materials available.
