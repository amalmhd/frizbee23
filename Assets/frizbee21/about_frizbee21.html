About The Event

After the nerving buzz of the pandemic, FrizBee'21 announces it's flamboyant arrival as the year's legendary flagship event of IEEE SB MESCE, continuing it's saga in '21. Bringing you it's fifth sequel, The All India level event is scheduled to be held from 17th to 19th of December 2021. In addition to the multitudinous workshops and inspiring talks, we make it our priority to provide a platform in enriching students to increase their skills and develop innovation.

MES College of Engineering, Kuttippuram
